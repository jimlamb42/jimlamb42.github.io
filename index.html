About me

With a BS in Digital Media Arts from Empire State College I have continued my work outside of college in creative design through audio, video, and digital graphics.

I have previously been a student at RIT, Buffalo State College, University at Buffalo and Purchase College. These experience have led me to have a diverse educational background and a range of coursework experience consistently centered around computer science, music production and digital art.

If you are interested in my goals for continuing my education you can find them through my Statement of Purpose.

My work experience has been primarily educational, with my current place of employment being the Buffalo and Erie County Public Library System where I instruct patrons on how to use audio and video production software. My previous work environment was running the Media Lab at SUNY Empire State College.
